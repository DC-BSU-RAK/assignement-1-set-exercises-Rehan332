Sourdough Bread

A simple recipe for homemade sourdough bread with a crispy crust and chewy interior.

Ingredients

  • 150g active sourdough starter
  • 500g bread flour
  • 350g water
  • 10g salt

Method

  1. Mix starter and water, then add flour and combine.
  2. Add salt after 30 minutes of rest.
  3. Perform stretch and folds every 30 minutes for 2 hours.
  4. Let dough rise for 2-3 more hours.
  5. Shape dough and place in a floured basket.
  6. Refrigerate overnight (12-15 hours).
  7. Preheat oven to 500Â°F with a Dutch oven inside.
  8. Score dough and bake covered for 20 minutes, then uncovered for 20-25 minutes.
  9. Cool completely before slicing.
