Pineapple Custard

This Pineapple Custard is a creamy, tropical dessert made with fresh pineapple and a smooth vanilla custard base. Itâ€™s quick to prepare and perfect for summer days or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped pineapple (fresh or canned)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp custard powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Chopped nuts (optional)
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. In a small bowl, mix custard powder with 1/4 cup of milk to make a smooth paste.
  2. Boil the remaining milk in a saucepan. Once hot, add sugar and stir well.
  3. Lower the heat and slowly add the custard paste, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook until the custard thickens, then remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  5. Add vanilla extract and mix in the pineapple chunks.
  6. Pour into serving bowls and chill in the fridge for at least an hour.
  7. Garnish with chopped nuts and mint leaves before serving.
